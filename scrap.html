Your Hosts

After getting married in Palisade in 2006, Cameron (a Brit) and Jen (a Colorado native) lived and worked in the UK for 10 years. They relocated to Palisade in 2015 and fell in love with this unique property. Now they, and golden retriever Alba, want to share it with you.

Accomodations

Guests at Palisade River Ranch have access to the trails across the main property, to the river and riverfront, and also the 1500 sq ft deck and screened gazebo down at the river. The riverfront and deck are great areas to enjoy a BBQ or campfire, to play games and to generally enjoy the serenity of our stretch of the Colorado. For those interested in floating the river, we have excellent put-in/take-out access. Guests can also explore our 45-acre riparian habitat, which is a great place to see an array of wildlife. (NB - access to some areas may be limited by seasonal restrictions, private parties or other events, and is subject to guests' agreement to a liability waiver).
