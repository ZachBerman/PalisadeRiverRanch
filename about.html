About The Ranch



Palisade River Ranch is home to a variety of wildlife and is located adjacent to the Town of Palisade's Riverbend Park. It is only a 10 min cruise by bicycle along the picturesque Riverfront Trail to visit the Downtown area of Palisade, and a 5 min walk to the event location for events such as Winefest, Bluegrass and Roots Festival, and the Peach Festival.

The ranch sits amongst a variety of vineyards, wineries, orchards and other agricultural attractions, with easy access to the Fruit and Wine Byway, and direct access to the Riverfront Trail and the Colorado River. The adjacent Riverbend Park is off-leash dog-friendly, has an excellent disc golf course and is another great place to exercise or picnic.

About The Area



The Grand Valley is full of outdoor activity and adventure opportunities, across alpine and desert environments, including some fantastic local hiking and biking trails. Guests can reach Powderhorn Ski Resort and the Grand Mesa in just over half an hour. Both locations provide countless opportunities to explore and to get involved with sports and activities across all seasons, including skiing and downhill mountain biking.

The Town of Grand Junction is a stone's throw away, with its historic Downtown and Main Street, and attractions for all ages and interests. Within half an hour to the east, guests can explore the spectacular National Monument and some more World-Class road and mountain biking routes and trails. Further afield, guests can reach places like Aspen, Glenwood Springs and Moab within 1 to 2 hours.
