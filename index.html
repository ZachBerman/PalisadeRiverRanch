About the Ranch

Palisade River Ranch is a beautiful 100-acre events and accommodation venue in the heart of Colorado Wine Country. With almost a mile of picturesque Colorado River frontage and stunning views of the surrounding mountains, the Ranch is a hidden gem which offers a tranquil and private setting for relaxing getaways, and for weddings and other events.

Guests have access to the trails across the Ranch and also to the deck and screened gazebo down by the river. The riverfront and deck are great areas to enjoy a BBQ or campfire, to play games and to generally enjoy the serenity of our stretch of the Colorado. For those interested in floating the river, we also have great put-in/take-out access. Guests can also explore our 45-acre riparian woodland, which is a great place to see an array of wildlife.
